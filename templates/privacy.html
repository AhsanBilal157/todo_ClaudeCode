Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Spendly, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Service. This includes:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as pages visited and actions taken, used to diagnose issues and improve the Service.
  • Device information — browser type and operating system, collected automatically when you access the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve performance and reliability.
  • To send you important service-related notices (not marketing emails).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We will never use your financial data to target you with ads.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed before storage and are never stored or transmitted in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

While we implement industry-standard safeguards, no system is completely immune to breaches. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you promptly via the email address on your account.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a limited number of third-party services to operate the platform (for example, hosting providers). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please reach out to us. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.

You can contact us via the support link in your account settings, or by emailing us directly at privacy@spendly.app.
